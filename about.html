About

WindFlow is the result of the work of people at the Parallel Programming Models group (PPMs), Department of Computer Science of the University of Pisa, Italy. The library is still under development to improve it and to provide additional features. The main authors or contributors are listed below:

  • Gabriele Mencagli (Associate Professor), main maintainer
  • Massimo Torquati (Assistant Professor), FastFlow's main maintainer
  • Tiziano De Matteis (Research Fellow), contributor
  • Alessandra Fais (PhD student), contributor
  • Elia Ruggeri (student), contributor
  • Andrea Cardaci (student), contributor
  • Cosimo Agati (student), contributor
  • Fausto Frasca (student), developer of the Web Dashboard
  • Alberto Ottimo (PhD student), contributor
  • Matteo Della Bartola (student), contributor

For any information regarding the library, please send and email to Gabriele Mencagli.
